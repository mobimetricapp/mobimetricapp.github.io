Privacy Policy for MobiMetric

At MobiMetric, we care about your privacy. This app is designed to be as transparent and secure as possible.

1. Personal Information: This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information (such as your name, email, or location).

2. Ads: We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads and provide a better experience.

3. Firebase Services: We use Firebase (a Google service) to improve app stability and monitor performance. Firebase may collect non-identifiable technical data, such as crash reports and basic device information, to help us fix bugs and improve the user experience.

4. Performance Data: All hardware and processor analysis (such as FPS and CPU tests) is performed locally on your device and is not sent to any external servers.

5. Contact: If you have any questions, contact us at: mobimetric.app@gmail.com

6. Consent: By using this app, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.
